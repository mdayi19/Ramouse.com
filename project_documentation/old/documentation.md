## Project Overview

**Ramouse Auto Parts** is a comprehensive platform connecting car owners and technicians with parts providers in Syria.

### Key Features:

*   **Smart Ordering**: Multi-step form with media uploads for accurate part requests.
*   **E-Commerce Store**: Dedicated section for oils, batteries, and accessories with category management.
*   **Service Directories**: Searchable directories for verified Technicians and Tow Truck operators with geolocation support.
*   **AI Assistant**: Gemini-powered diagnostics and vehicle information.
*   **Provider Bidding**: Competitive quoting system for parts.
*   **Financial System**: Wallet and withdrawal management for providers.
*   **Multi-Channel Notifications**: Integrated WhatsApp and Telegram notifications.
*   **Admin Control**: Full CMS for users, orders, content (Blog/FAQ), and system settings.
